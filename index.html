The LocalRoots way

How LocalRoots works

Trading with your neighbors has always been one of the most natural things in the world. We just made it easier.

1
Sign up in 30 seconds

Just your name, email, and town. No payment details, no subscriptions, no catch. Free forever.

2
Post what you have, browse what's there

Got tomatoes, a skill, or something gathering dust? Post it in seconds. See what neighbors are offering. Every listing shows exactly what they want in return.

3
Message, meet, and make the trade

Message directly — no platform in the middle. Agree a time and place, make the swap, and leave a short review. Over time your trust score tells the community exactly who you are.

Why we exist

Our mission

To rebuild the kind of communities where neighbors actually know each other and trade is just one way of getting there.

"Somewhere along the way, we stopped knowing our neighbors."

We live closer to more people than any generation in history and yet most of us don't know the names of the people next door. We buy everything from strangers online, throw away things that others would treasure, and pay for skills that someone three streets over would happily trade for a home-cooked meal.

LocalRoots was built because we believe that's not inevitable. It's just a problem nobody built a good solution for.

The platform is simple by design. No algorithms deciding what you see. No ads steering your attention. Trading freely between neighbors. Just a clean, honest space where you can post what you have, find what you need, and deal directly with a real person in your community.

We think the future looks more local, not less. As the cost of everything rises, as supply chains prove fragile, and as people increasingly crave genuine human connection, the ability to trade with your neighbors becomes not just pleasant but genuinely valuable.

LocalRoots is free. It will always be free. We are not building this to extract value from communities we're building it to give something back to them.

Our goal is simple: that every person who joins LocalRoots makes at least one trade that makes their life a little better and meets at least one neighbor they're glad they know.

Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Written in plain English, not legalese.

What we collect

When you create an account we collect your name, email address, and the city or town you choose to share. When you post a listing we store the content of that listing. When you send a message we store that message so both parties can read it. That's it.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your precise location, phone number, payment information, or any financial data. We do not track you across other websites. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.

How we use your data

Your name and city are shown on your public profile so other members can know who they're trading with. Your email is used only to send you account-related communications confirmation emails, password resets, and (if you opt in) notifications when someone messages you. We do not send marketing emails.

Who can see your information

Your name, city, listings, and reviews are visible to other LocalRoots members. Your email address is never shown publicly. Your messages are private between you and the person you're messaging.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted infrastructure provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Your rights

You can update or delete your account at any time from your profile settings. If you would like all of your data permanently deleted, contact us and we will action it within 30 days. You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you.

Cookies

LocalRoots uses only essential cookies required for you to stay logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy we will notify members by email and update the date at the top of this page. We will never make changes that reduce your privacy rights without clearly telling you first.

Questions about your privacy? Reach out at contact us we'll reply personally, not with an automated response.

Your safety matters

Trust & Safety

LocalRoots is built on real human connection. These guidelines help keep every trade safe, fair, and genuinely good for everyone involved.

🤝
Before your first trade
1.
Check their profile
Look at how long they have been a member, how many trades they have completed, and what reviews they have received.
2.
Agree everything in messages first
Use LocalRoots messaging to agree exactly what you are swapping, when, and where. Both parties have a clear record.
3.
Trust your instincts
If something feels off, trust that feeling and do not proceed.
📍
Meeting up safely
Meet in public farmers markets, coffee shop parking lots, outside a library or grocery store.
Bring a friend for your first few trades if it makes you more comfortable.
Daytime is better for initial meetups with someone new.
Never share your home address until you are confident in the person you are trading with.
🌱
Trading food safely
Be honest about freshness, how it was grown, and any allergens in homemade goods.
If trading homemade food, mention key ingredients so people with allergies can decide.
Do not offer anything you would not feel comfortable giving to a good friend.
🚩
When something goes wrong

If you experience a misleading listing, aggressive behavior, or anything concerning, please report it. We take every report seriously and accounts that violate community standards are removed.

Report a safety concern
Email us at contact us and we will respond personally.
